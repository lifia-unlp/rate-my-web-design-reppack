### Screen 2: Disclaimer

_Dear X,_
_You are going to take part in an assessment of the generative AI applicability in creating web user interfaces designs. Gen AI models can soon become an essential aid to the people of creative industries, so we want to know how useful they are currently for the professional designers._

_Please, pay attention to the following:_

_We are NOT testing YOU, and your evaluations can't be good or bad. Still, we would appreciate if you provide them responsibly._
_In each trial, you will be presented with a website design prototype screenshot. We recommend clicking on the screenshot (it will open in a tab), spend 5-10 seconds examining it, and then provide your SUBJECTIVE assessments per the scales below._
_We are assessing the prototypes of the website DESIGNS, so please try to disregard their content and topic in your evaluations._
_During the survey, we plan to collect some of your personal data and e-mail address (they won't be shared with any third party). By clicking the button below, you indicate that you agree and are willing to take part in the survey._
